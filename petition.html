The National Case

Why Israel. Why Now. Why Ibogaine.

Israel has long been a world leader in medical innovation — from cannabis research to battlefield medicine. Today, a new frontier demands our attention: ibogaine, a natural psychoactive compound derived from the African iboga plant, is showing extraordinary promise for PTSD, addiction, traumatic brain injury, and treatment-resistant depression.

After October 7th, our nation faces an unprecedented mental health crisis. Tens of thousands of combat veterans, former hostages, and civilians carry invisible wounds. Conventional treatments are failing them. Ibogaine is not.

In 2023, Stanford University published a landmark study showing that a single ibogaine treatment, combined with magnesium, dramatically reduced PTSD, depression, and anxiety in special operations veterans. Texas moved first. Now Israel must lead.

01 / Veterans & Trauma

Thousands of IDF soldiers and October 7th survivors urgently need treatments that work beyond SSRIs and talk therapy.

02 / Addiction Crisis

Ibogaine has shown the ability to interrupt opioid and alcohol dependence, in some cases after a single treatment session.

03 / Scientific Leadership

Israel's research ecosystem — world-class hospitals, biotech infrastructure, and regulatory agility — is uniquely positioned to lead.

04 / Economic Opportunity

Becoming the global hub for ibogaine treatment and IP development could generate billions in medical tourism and biotech exports.

The Texas Model

A Practical Legislative Blueprint

In 2023, Texas passed HB 1802 — a first-of-its-kind bill establishing a research program on ibogaine and other psychedelic compounds for treating veterans. We call for a Knesset bill that goes further, establishing Israel as the definitive global leader.

  • State-funded clinical research program
  • Regulated ibogaine treatment centers
  • Priority access for IDF veterans
  • International research partnerships
  • IP framework to anchor global industry
  • Medical training and certification pipeline
